                                    http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/28507176?tool=bestpractice.com
                                    
                                    
                                 

                                 

                                    
                                    Intubation and mechanical ventilation may be necessary for patients with severe hypoxaemia/respiratory failure. Mechanical ventilation can lead to hypotension, so blood pressure (BP) should be monitored closely.
